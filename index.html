About the show

Design, leadership &
the stuff nobody tells you.

Ship Now, Cry Later is the podcast for designers and design leaders navigating the messy, exciting, sometimes chaotic reality of building great products. We talk about the hard calls, the unexpected growth moments, and the lessons that only come from doing the actual work. Roots before branches — always.
